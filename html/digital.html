Digital Marketing FAQs 2025

1. What is Digital Marketing?
Digital Marketing is the promotion of products or services using digital channels such as search engines, social media, email, and websites.
2. Why is SEO important?
SEO helps improve the visibility of a website in search engine results, driving organic traffic and improving brand credibility.
3. What is Social Media Marketing?
Social Media Marketing involves creating content and advertisements on platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn to engage the audience and grow the brand.
4. What is PPC advertising?
PPC (Pay-Per-Click) advertising is where advertisers pay a fee each time their ad is clicked, commonly seen in Google Ads and social media campaigns.
5. How to measure digital marketing success?
Success can be measured using KPIs such as website traffic, conversion rates, social media engagement, ROI, and lead generation.
